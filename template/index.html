Fake News Detection
Machine Learning

Detection of fake news online is important in today's society as fresh news content is rapidly being produced as a result of the abundance of available technology. Claire Wardle has identified seven main categories of fake news, and within each category, the fake news content can be either visual and/or linguistic-based. In order to detect fake news, both linguistic and non-linguistic cues can be analyzed using several methods.
